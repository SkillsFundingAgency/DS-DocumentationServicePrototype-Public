Guidance

Submitting Individualised learner record data and guidance to meet the requirements (ILR)

Give feedback about this page

Published by:
Education and Skills Funding Agency
Last updated:
31 January 2019

If you receive funding directly from the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) you need to submit individualised learner records (ILRs). ESFA uses this to calculate the funding earned by providers.

  1. Specification of the Individualised learner record (ILR) 2019 to 2020

    A technical specification document of the data collection requirements and file format of the ILR, used to enable learning and training providers to to meet the requirements for ILR data returns in 2019 to 2020.

  2. Provider Support Manual for 2018 to 2019 (Version 1)

    Guidance to help providers meet the requirements for individualised learner records (ILR) data returns 2018 to 2019.
  3. ILR validation rules

    Technical document aimed at those responsible for making data returns; data specification implementation; and MI system design (including MI managers, commercial software suppliers and own software writers)
  4. Appendices

    These documents support further education providers (publicly funded colleges, training organisations, local authorities and employers) to understand the details of the data about learners and their learning that must be collected.
