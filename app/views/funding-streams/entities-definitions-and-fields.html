Overview

Entities covered by the ILR

  1. Learning Provider

    This entity collects data about the provider.
  2. Learner

    This entity collects attributes that relate to an individual learner such as their name and date of birth.
  3. Learner Contact Preference

    This entity collects any restrictions on the use of the learner's contact details and any restrictions on how a learner wishes to be contacted.
  4. LLDD and Health Problem

    This entity collect details of the nature of the learner's disability, learning difficulty and/or health problem.
  5. Learner Funding and Monitoring (FAM)

    This entity collects additional attributes of the learner that will inform either the funding of the learner or additional monitoring.
  6. Learner Provider Specified Monitoring

    This entity collects data specified by the provider for their own use.
  7. Learner Employment Status

    This entity collects the details of a learner's employment status before they start their learning (prior to enrolment) and any changes during their programme of learning.
  8. Learner HE

    This entity collects data that is requested by the Office for Students (OfS) and the Higher Education Statistics Agency (HESA).
  9. Learner HE Financial Support

    This entity collects data about the financial support received by students.
  10. Learning Delivery

    This entity collects details of the learning aims that a learner is studying. A learning delivery record should be returned for each learning aim that a learner is studying.
  11. Learning Delivery Funding and Monitoring (FAM)

    This entity collects additional attributes of the Learning Delivery that will either inform funding of the learner or additional monitoring.
  12. Learning Delivery Work Placement

    This entity collects data about a work placement that is being undertaken by a learner as part of a traineeship or 16-19 study programme.
  13. Apprenticeship Financial Record

    This entity collects data about the cost of an apprenticeship and financial contributions received from an employer for an apprenticeship.
  14. Apprenticeship Financial Type – AfinType

    The type of financial details being recorded.
  15. Provider Spec Delivery Monitoring

    This entity collects data specified by the provider for their own use.
  16. Learning Delivery HE

    Data requested by OfS and HESA.
  17. Learner Destination and Progression

    This entity records destination and progression outcomes for a learner, such as gaining employment or going onto further study. These outcomes will be reported after a learner has completed a programme of learning.
